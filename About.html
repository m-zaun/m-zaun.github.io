About Me

My name is Michael Zaun and I'm a travel photographer. On this website you can find some pictures I've taken during my travels. I live in Chicago, IL, where I am an Investment Consultant by day, Kellogg student by night and coder by weekend. I have been taking pictures over the past ten years when I bought my first DSLR. I tend to spend a lot of my free time traveling. I love the stunning architecture of Istanbul, the great expanse of the Western United States, as well as modern metropolitan areas. After visiting major spots in Europe and the United States, I have started to explore South America. Stay in touch!
