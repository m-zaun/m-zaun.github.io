Summary

Michael studied at the University of Alaska, where he proved himself a brilliant accountant with a specialty in debits and credits. Following his education, Michael went on to co-found Accounting Matters Technologies with his friend, Mitch Worden. He now lives in Chicago and attends Kellogg.
